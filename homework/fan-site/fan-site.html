Touhou Project Fan Site!


Brief Overview:

東方Project, The Touhou Project, or simply Touhou is a series of bullet hells among other various spinoffs developed by Team Shanghai Alice.
Team Shanghai Alice consists of a single developer, known as ZUN. The series of over 19 games is incredibly popular, and yet simultaneously unheard of.
While most of the games are bullet hells developed by ZUN, a good chunk of spinoffs are collaborations with a small studio known as Twilight Frontier.
The games are heavily inspired by Japanese mythology, with most of the characters being humanizations of Youkai.
Apologies for the wikipedia-style synopsis but a brief overview on the front page was at least needed for context, go to the history page for more info!
